  • High Availability Techniques:

    To ensure high availability of database services, I learned about and implemented techniques such as

    • database mirroring
    • Always On availability
    • clustering
    . These techniques are crucial for minimizing downtime and ensuring data redundancy. Clustering provides a way to group multiple servers to work together, ensuring that if one server fails, another can take over seamlessly.
